About Us

With campuses across Venezuela, UNES combines rigorous academic instruction with hands-on practical training, preparing students to meet the challenges of real-world security scenarios. Our facilities include state-of-the-art training centers where students can simulate emergency responses, crime prevention strategies, and public safety initiatives. We are committed to advancing public security through research and policy development, working closely with national and international organizations to improve the standards and effectiveness of security forces. At UNES, we believe in fostering a new generation of security professionals who are not only skilled but deeply committed to the well-being of their communities. Objective

We aim to foster a security system that prioritizes human rights, ethical conduct, and community service, ensuring that our graduates contribute to a safer and more just society. Through innovative academic programs, practical training, and research, we strive to develop skilled professionals capable of addressing the evolving challenges in public safety, crime prevention, and disaster management, while upholding the highest standards of transparency, accountability, and social responsibility.
