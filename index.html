About university

UNES (Universidad Nacional Experimental de la Seguridad) is a public university in Venezuela, specializing in security sciences and public safety. It was established in 2009 to train professionals in fields such as policing, firefighting, criminal investigation, and disaster management, with a strong focus on human rights and community service. It also engages in research and policy development to enhance security practices and address public safety issues in the country.

UNES offers a range of undergraduate and graduate programs focusing on public security, criminalistics, firefighting, civil protection, and even penitentiary services. Its curricula integrate both theoretical education and practical training, ensuring students gain hands-on experience in their respective fields. The programs emphasize human rights, citizen protection, and the democratization of security, which is vital for community-based approaches to policing and crime prevention.
